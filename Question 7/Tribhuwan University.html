Faculties and Programs at Tribhuvan University (TU)

Tribhuvan University (TU) offers various programs under different faculties. Below is a list of the main faculties and some of the programs available within them.

Faculty of Management

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Business Studies (MBS)
  • Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)
  • Master of Public Administration (MPA)

Faculty of Science

  • Bachelor of Science (BSc) in various disciplines (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics)
  • Master of Science (MSc) in various disciplines
  • Bachelor of Computer Information Systems (BCIS)
  • Master of Computer Information Systems (MCIS)
  • Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT)

Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences

  • Bachelor of Arts (BA) in subjects like English, Nepali, History, Sociology, etc.
  • Master of Arts (MA) in various humanities subjects
  • Bachelor of Education (BEd)
  • Master of Education (MEd)
  • Bachelor of Social Work (BSW)
